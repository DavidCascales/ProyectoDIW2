How We Works

Where do we get the materials?

We source materials through a network of sustainable product collection points, including recyclable plastics and other reusable waste. We collaborate with communities, businesses, and recycling centers to ensure a continuous and responsible supply.

How do we recycle the materials?

The collected materials go through a process of sorting, cleaning, and processing to transform them into filaments and other raw materials suitable for 3D printing. This process minimizes environmental impact and maximizes the quality of the recycled material.

Use of materials for 3D printing

The recycled materials are converted into high-quality filaments that power our 3D printing machines. These filaments are used to create innovative and functional products, promoting circular economy and sustainability.

You can print your 3D models at our locations using recycled materials. Join us in promoting sustainability and innovation!
